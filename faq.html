FAQ

How do I book an appointment?: Through emailing me at maxmotocarellc@gmail.com. Make sure to send your home address, first and last name, phone number, year, make, model, trim of your vehicle, and the services you'd like to get done. I will reply with an estimate and an estimated time of completion for the services. Once I've confirmed everything with the customer and ordered parts, refunds are not available.

Where are services performed?:Right in my garage to ensure your vehicles' safety, to comply with HOA, and to keep your driveway clean. I use absorbent mats in my garage to ensure no environmental hazards

Where are the old parts/oil disposed of?:All parts/oil are disposed of through government-approved Hazardous Waste Centers to ensure environmental safety

Payment policy:Once parts are ordered, refunds are not available. I will confirm and finalize the estimate with you before anything is ordered
